Ring Exclusive: Sources reveal Canelo Alvarez-Jermall Charlo on May 4 in Vegas

Canelo Álvarez will defend his super middleweight titles against Jermall Charlo
on May 4. The fight holds significance as Charlo seeks revenge for his brother
Jermell's defeat to Álvarez. Álvarez's status with PBC remains secure, despite
rumors of departure.
