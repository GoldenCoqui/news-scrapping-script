Clear and present: Oscar De La Hoya vs. Ike Quartey

Oscar De La Hoya faced a significant challenge against Ike Quartey, a powerful
and skilled fighter. Despite concerns about Quartey's threat, De La Hoya
displayed courage and resilience throughout the 12-round battle. While Quartey
controlled the early rounds, De La Hoya's superior movement and increasing punch
output turned the tide. In the championship rounds, De La Hoya relentlessly
attacked and floored Quartey twice, securing a hard-fought victory and proving
his exceptional talent.
