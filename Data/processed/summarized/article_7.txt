Seniesa Estrada’s 13-Year Journey To The Sport’s Brightest Stage

Seniesa Estrada, despite early setbacks, has become a pound-for-pound champion
and the 105-pound Ring champion. Estrada's journey mirrors the evolution of
women's boxing, which has seen increased popularity and recognition. Estrada's
fight against Yokasta Valle for the undisputed championship headlines a growing
trend of women's boxing gaining mainstream attention and compensating fighters
fairly.
