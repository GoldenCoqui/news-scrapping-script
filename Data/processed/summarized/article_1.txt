Victor Morales-Luis Nuñez scheduled as co-main event of Zepeda-Hughes on March 16

Two undefeated featherweights, Victor Morales and Luis Nuñez, will face off in a
12-round bout on March 16 in Las Vegas. The fight will serve as a WBA final
eliminator, with the winner becoming the mandatory challenger for the vacant
world title. Both fighters have impressive records with Morales holding a slight
edge in knockouts. The match is expected to be a competitive and entertaining
contest.
