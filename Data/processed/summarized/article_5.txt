Ring Exclusive: Sources reveal Canelo Alvarez-Jermall Charlo on May 4 in Vegas

Canelo Alvarez will reportedly defend his undisputed super middleweight titles
against Jermall Charlo on May 4 in Las Vegas. Jermall seeks revenge for his
brother, Jermell's, loss to Alvarez in 2023. Despite rumors, Alvarez is expected
to remain with PBC and defend his titles against Charlo.
