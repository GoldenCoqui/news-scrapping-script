Austin Brooks remains unbeaten after stopping Jose Izaguirre in seven rounds

Austin Brooks emerged as a prospect in the 130-pound division after defeating
Jose Izaguirre in round seven. In a clash of unbeaten southpaws, Brooks overcame
a slow start to gain momentum and hurt Izaguirre in the sixth round. A series of
punches to the body dropped Izaguirre in round seven, giving Brooks the victory.
