Eye of the Tiger signs Shamil Khataev and Arthur Biyarslanov

Eye of the Tiger has added undefeated middleweight Shamil Khataev and junior
welterweight Arthur Biyarslanov to its promotional stable. The Montreal-based
outfit now has 22 fighters under contract, including several world-ranked
contenders. Khataev (12-0, 3 KOs) has fought at home in Russia and recently won
a WBC regional title. Biyarslanov (13-0, 11 KOs), a former Canadian Olympian,
has fought in Canada and America. Both fighters will make their EOTT debuts on a
date and venue to be announced.
