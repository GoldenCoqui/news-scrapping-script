Victor Morales-Luis Nuñez scheduled as co-main event of Zepeda-Hughes on March 16

On March 16, unbeaten featherweights Victor Morales and Luis Nuñez will face off
in a WBA title eliminator. Both fighters are ranked in the top 10 and the bout
is expected to be competitive. Morales, from Vancouver, Washington, has won four
straight fights since a draw in 2020. Nuñez, from the Dominican Republic, has
defeated three unbeaten fighters in the past year. The winner of this bout could
become a mandatory challenger for the vacant WBA featherweight title.
