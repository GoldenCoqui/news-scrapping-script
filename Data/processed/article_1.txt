Headline: Headline: Eye of the Tiger signs Shamil Khataev and Arthur Biyarslanov

Eye of The Tiger has signed two undefeated prospects: middleweight Shamil
Khataev and junior welterweight Arthur Biyarslanov, adding to their growing
stable of 22 fighters. Estephan, Eye of The Tiger's head, emphasizes their
strong talent roster and plans for significant fights. Khataev, a 12-0 fighter
from Russia, holds a regional WBC title. Biyarslanov, a former Olympian
representing Canada, brings an impressive 13-0 record with 11 knockouts. The
fighters, who are both hardworking professionals, will make their EOTT debuts at
a later date.
