Austin Brooks remains unbeaten after stopping Jose Izaguirre in seven rounds

Austin Brooks, a rising 130-pound prospect, triumphed over Jose Izaguirre via
seventh-round knockout. Brooks overcame Izaguirre's early dominance and gained
momentum in the fourth round. In the sixth round, he damaged Izaguirre, leading
to a body shot knockdown in the seventh. In the co-main event, Tayden Beltran
defeated Willmank Canonico Brito by unanimous decision. Hector Lopez also
secured a second-round stoppage victory over Rod Saraguilla.
