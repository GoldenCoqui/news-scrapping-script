Eye of the Tiger signs Shamil Khataev and Arthur Biyarslanov

Eye of the Tiger Promotions has signed undefeated middleweight Shamil Khataev
(12-0, 3 KOs) and junior welterweight Arthur Biyarslanov (13-0, 11 KOs). Khataev
has fought primarily in Russia and holds a regional WBC title. Biyarslanov, a
former Canadian Olympian, has fought both in Canada and the United States. The
signings bring Eye of the Tiger's roster to 22 fighters, strengthening the
promotion's position in the middleweight and junior welterweight divisions.
