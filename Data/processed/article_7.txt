Seniesa Estrada’s 13-Year Journey To The Sport’s Brightest Stage

Seniesa Estrada, the current Ring 105-pound champion, has faced numerous
obstacles in her career but is now one win away from the undisputed
championship. Her journey reflects the rise of women's boxing, which has gained
prominence and compensation. Estrada's upcoming fight against Costa Rican
Yokasta Valle will crown the first-ever undisputed strawweight champion. Women's
boxing has evolved from a niche sport to a golden era, thanks to fighters like
Estrada, Valle, and others who have raised their profiles and proven their
skills.
