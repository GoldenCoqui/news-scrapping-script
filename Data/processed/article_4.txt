Clear and present: Oscar De La Hoya vs. Ike Quartey

Oscar De La Hoya faced a serious threat when he fought Ike Quartey in 1999.
Despite Quartey's initial dominance, De La Hoya adapted his strategy, using his
movement and punch output to gain control. In the final rounds, De La Hoya
unleashed a relentless attack, knocking Quartey down twice and securing a hard-
fought victory.
